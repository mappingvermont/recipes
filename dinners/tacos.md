## Tacos! :taco:

Source: [Cookie and Kate](https://cookieandkate.com/sweet-potato-black-bean-tacos/)

### Ingredients

- 2 lbs Covington sweet potatoes
- 2 cans black beans, drained
- 1 red pepper
- 1 yellow pepper
- 1 red onion
- 1 small yellow onion
- 1 tsp lime juice
- Olive oil
- Cumin
- Chili powder
- Taco cheese
- Chorizo (optional)
- Plenty of greek yogurt
- Tortillas


### Instructions

- Preheat oven to 425°. Peel and dice sweet potatoes. Toss with 2 tbsp olive oil and 1/4 tsp salt. Bake 20 - 25 minutes, tossing every 5 minutes to prevent burning.

- Dice the small yellow onion. Cook over medium heat with 1 tbsp olive oil and a little salt until translucent. Add 2 tsp cumin and 1/4 tsp chili powder. Cook for 30 seconds. Rinse and drain two cans black beans. Add them with 1/3 c water. Simmer with lid on for 5 minutes. Remove from heat and mash. Add 1 tsp lime juice. Season with salt (1/4 tsp) and pepper.

- Cut red onion and peppers into thin strips. Sauté in a frying pan with olive oil.

- Cook chorizo if using.

- Heat tortillas in a cast iron

- Eat!
